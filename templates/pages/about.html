About SkillCore

We're on a mission to bring transparency to the mobile repair industry.

Our Story

Finding a reliable repair shop for your phone shouldn't be a hassle. You shouldn't have to call ten different shops just to find out how much it costs to fix your screen. That's why we built SkillCore.

SkillCore is Sweden's first comprehensive comparison platform for mobile repairs. We connect users with verified local repair shops, making it easy to compare prices, read reviews, and find the best service for your device.

We are not a repair shop. We don't sell parts or fix phones ourselves. Our sole purpose is to help you find the right professional for the job at the right price.
